Elevation Certificate

Before FEMA can issue a Letter of Map Amendment (LOMA) or a Letter of Map Revision to remove a property and/or structure from the SFHA, the applicant will need an Elevation Certificate for the property.

Arrington Engineering can quickly prepare an Elevation Certificate for your property located in Shelby County, AL including Alabaster, Birmingham, Calera, Chelsea, Columbiana, Harpersville, Helena, Hoover, Leeds, Montevallo, Pelham, Vestavia Hills, Vincent, Westover, Wilsonville or Wilton.

Mortgage Surveys

Mortgage Survey is the term used to describe surveys required by the lender in order to provide financing.

The purpose of a mortgage survey is to show any improvements or encroachments on a piece of land.

Quarry Inventory Control Surveys

Quarries must maintain an inventory of the volumes of different sizes of quarried stone and crushed aggregate and sands stockpiled on site at a given date. This inventory has become increasingly important since the passing of the Sarbane- Oxley law in 2002 which established new accountability standards for corporate management.

Arrington Engineering can safely, efficiently, and accurately provide stockpile volumes without interfering with production. By utilizing a high definition laser scanner or a robotic instrument we can survey the stockpile without walking on the pile.

Civil Engineering

FEMA Permitting

In 1968 Congress passed the National Flood Insurance Act, which created the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP).

Lands that are at risk of being flooded are called Special Flood Hazard Areas (SFHA) and are subject to special flood hazard insurance. The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) has implemented procedures for requesting revisions to NFIP maps.

Arrington Engineering is experienced with FEMA procedures and can submit the required paperwork for legitimate map amendment request.

NPDES Permitting and Inspections

The National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NDPES) Permit Program mandates by Federal Law that all Construction sites of 1 acre or greater be registered. This registration requires that a plan be prepared for the discharge of stormwater from the site. This plan is referred to as a Construction Best Management Practices Plan (CBMPP).

Arrington Engineering Can prepare the NDPES permit application and perform the required inspections and reporting to assure that your project stays in compliance.
